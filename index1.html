What We Do

Our Core Services

From raw grey fabric to finished, vibrant textile — we handle every stage of processing with uncompromising precision and care.

🧼

Bleaching

Professional bleaching achieving pristine whiteness while preserving fabric integrity. Suitable for cotton, polyester blends, and synthetic fabrics using eco-approved agents.

🎨

Dyeing

Vibrant, long-lasting dyeing across the full colour spectrum using reactive, vat, and disperse dyes with computerised colour-matching for exact Pantone specifications.

🖨️

Printing

Screen, rotary, and digital printing for complex patterns. Our printing lines deliver sharp, vivid prints on all fabric types with exceptional wash fastness.

✨

Finishing

Calendering, mercerizing, sanforizing, anti-wrinkle, and softening treatments — enhancing texture, feel, and performance characteristics of every fabric.

🏭

Fabric Processing

End-to-end grey fabric processing including pre-treatment, scouring, and heat-setting. We handle bulk orders with consistent quality and fast turnaround times.

🔬

Quality Testing

In-house lab with advanced instruments for colour fastness, tensile strength, shrinkage, and dimensional stability. Every lot is certified before dispatch.

How It Works

Our Process

A proven, step-by-step workflow ensuring quality and traceability at every stage.

📦
Step 01

Fabric Intake

Fabric received, inspected for weight and construction, tagged and logged into our tracking system.

🧪
Step 02

Pre-treatment

Scouring, singeing, and desizing to remove impurities, preparing fabric for uniform processing.

🎨
Step 03

Processing

Bleaching, dyeing, or printing with precision-controlled machines calibrated per fabric type.

🔬
Step 04

Quality Check

Multi-point lab testing including shade, fastness, and shrinkage. Non-conforming batches reprocessed.

🚚
Step 05

Delivery

Finished fabric packed, labelled, and dispatched with quality certificate. On-time delivery guaranteed.
