About Us

Crafting Excellence in Every Fibre

Founded in 1998, Prem Pooja Textile Processors Pvt. Ltd. has grown from a single-unit operation into one of Maharashtra's most trusted textile processing companies — with state-of-the-art facilities in Mumbai and Badlapur.

We have spent over two decades refining our processes, investing in modern technology, and building lasting relationships with garment manufacturers, export houses, and fabric wholesalers. Our commitment is simple: deliver consistent quality, on time, every time.

Our skilled technicians and processing experts work with a diverse range of fabrics — from delicate cottons and silks to heavy industrial textiles — ensuring every batch meets the strictest quality benchmarks before it leaves our facility.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver superior textile processing services that empower manufacturers with quality, efficiency, and reliability.

🔭 Our Vision

To be the leading textile processing partner in India, setting benchmarks in innovation and sustainable practices.

💎 Quality First

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality testing before delivery — zero compromise on standards.

How It Works

Our Process

A proven, step-by-step workflow ensuring quality and traceability at every stage of processing.

STEP 01📦

Fabric Intake

Client fabric received, inspected for weight and construction. Each lot is tagged and logged into our tracking system.

STEP 02🧪

Pre-treatment

Scouring, singeing, and desizing to remove impurities and sizing agents — preparing the fabric for uniform processing.

STEP 03🎨

Processing

Bleaching, dyeing, or printing performed using precision-controlled machines with calibrated parameters per fabric type.

STEP 04🔬

Quality Check

Multi-point lab testing including shade, fastness, shrinkage, and physical properties. Non-conforming batches are reprocessed.

STEP 05🚚

Delivery

Finished fabric is packed, labelled, and dispatched with a quality certificate. On-time delivery is guaranteed.
